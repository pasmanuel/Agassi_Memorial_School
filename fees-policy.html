Fees Policy

School Fees Payment

  • Initial Payment: Upon reopening of the school, parents are required to pay at least half of the school fees.

  • Remaining Payment: The remaining half of the fees should be paid after the mid-term holidays.

  • Payment Methods: Fees can be paid through Yaa Asantewaa Rural Bank, Ejisu, or the School's MTN Merchant line (930736).

Canteen Fees

  • Inclusion: Canteen fees are included in the overall school fees.

Transportation Fares

  • Exclusion from School Fees: Transportation fares are not included in the school fees.

  • Payment Options: Transport fares can be paid daily, weekly, monthly, or termly, depending on the parents' convenience.

Examination Fees

  • Exclusion from School Fees: Examination fees are not included in the school fees.

  • Timing: Examination fees are charged after the mid-term holidays.

  • Basis:Fees are based on the market prices of printing materials..

School Uniforms

  • Separate Cost:School uniforms are obtainable from the school at an additional cost.

  • Types of Uniforms

    • Normal School Wear(designed)

    • Wednesday Wear(designed)

    • Friday Wear(designed)

  • Purchase:Parents/guardians need to buy three different uniforms.

Textbooks

  • Supply: Textbooks are supplied at the beginning of each academic year by the publishers/distributors.

Summary

  • Initial Fee Payment: Half of the fees upon school reopening.

  • Final Fee Payment: Examination fees are Remaining half after mid-term holidays.

  • Transportation: Separate and paid according to convenience.

  • Examination Fees: Separate and paid after mid-term holidays.

  • Uniforms: Separate cost, three types to be purchased.

  • Textbooks: Provided at the beginning of the academic year.
