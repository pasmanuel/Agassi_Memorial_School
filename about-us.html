About Us

DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE

The world has been founded on knowledge, learning and education. The ancient empires of Greece, Rome, Egypt, Ghana, Songhai, etc. thrived on the knowledge of its Kings and Leaders. In the civilization of the world, mankind has seen greater transformation in science, religion and culture due to the knowledge of its great men and women. Today, no country can develop and make life better for its citizens if the people remain uneducated and primitive. It is therefore the duty of every adult to see to the education of his/her young ones to enable them grow into enlightened and responsible adults who are determined to shape their own lives, the lives of their nation and the world.

AGASSI MEMORIAL SCHOOL helps you to achieve this by introducing into its curriculum, in addition to the National curriculum, specialized subjects, programmes and courses which will bring out the full potential of the child into a responsible and productive adult and citizen abreast with the trends in the modern world. The concerns and welfare of our staff, parents and students are dear to us. This makes Agassi one unique family

We entreat you to join the Agassi Family as a Parent or Staff for a wonderful experience.

Our Mission & Vision

OUR MISSION

At our school, our mission is to empower students to achieve their full potential through a comprehensive and engaging educational experience. We strive to foster a love for learning, encourage critical thinking, and cultivate a sense of responsibility and community. Our dedicated staff works collaboratively to create a nurturing and inclusive environment where every student is valued and supported.

OUR VISION

To be a leading educational institution that inspires and equips students to be lifelong learners and compassionate global citizens. We envision a school where innovation, creativity, and excellence are at the forefront of our teaching practices, and where students are prepared to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing world with confidence and competence.
