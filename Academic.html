ACADEMIC PROGRAMME

PRE-SCHOOL

Agassi Memorial School Montessori program focuses on leveraging innate childhood traits to develop a child's full potential.

These traits include curiosity, creativity, self-discovery, the need for order, and the desire to respond to sensorial input.

Our prime role is that the journey of our children is planned meticulously in order to provide an excellent foundation to foster positive attitude towards learners..

At Agassi our education is child-centered, with curriculum developed around the needs and abilities of the children.

PRIMARY

Our Primary level of education consists of classes from Basic Stage (B.S.) 1 to 6 and a class size of thirty (30).

JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL

The Junior High School is a three-year program that prepares students for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) leading to the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE).


The subjects offered for the course are:

  • English Language
  • Social Studies
  • Mathematics
  • Integrated Science
  • Computing / ICT
  • Ghanaian Language (Twi)
  • French
  • Computer Programing

The JHS department has highly competent graduate professional teachers handling these subjects.

SMARTRIBE

The Smartribe program features Computer programming, Robotics, Abacus, and Verdic Maths.
