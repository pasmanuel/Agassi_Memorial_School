Our Primary School

At Agassi Memorial School, our primary school program builds on the strong foundation established in preschool, guiding students through the crucial early years of formal education. Our curriculum is designed to cultivate a love of learning, critical thinking skills, and a sense of responsibility. We focus on the holistic development of each child, balancing academic excellence with emotional and social growth. Our dedicated teachers create a supportive and stimulating environment where students are encouraged to explore their interests and achieve their full potential.

Our Primary School Programs

Our primary school program at Agassi Memorial School covers a broad range of subjects, ensuring a well-rounded education for our students:

  • Numeracy: Developing strong mathematical skills and understanding.
  • Language and Literacy: Fostering reading, writing, and communication skills.
  • Science: Encouraging curiosity and scientific thinking through experiments and exploration.
  • History: Teaching about the past to understand the present and shape the future.
  • Our World, Our People: Exploring social studies and global awareness.
  • Creative Arts: Nurturing creativity through art, music, and drama.
  • Physical Education: Promoting physical health and teamwork through various sports and activities.
  • Ghanaian Language (Twi): Preserving and celebrating local languages and cultures.
  • French: Introducing a second language to broaden communication skills.
  • Abacus: Enhancing mathematical abilities through traditional calculation techniques.
  • Computer Programming: Preparing students for the digital age with coding and technology skills.
