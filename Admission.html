Admissions Guide

PRESCHOOL

Step 1: Contact the Headteacher

A prospective applicant/parent who intends to enroll his/her ward into our Pre-School must contact the Headteacher by visiting the School.

Step 2: Submission of Documents

The applicant/parent must come along with the birth certificate of the child or any document that validly proves the name and age of the child to conclude the admission process.

Step 3: Payment of Fees

The parent/guardian is required to pay the full fees for the chosen class. Note that this payment does not cover school uniforms, books, and transport fare.

PRIMARY-JHS

Step 1: Contact the Headteacher

The prospective applicant or parent should visit Agassi Memorial School to contact the Headteacher.

Step 2: Entrance Examination

The child intending to enroll in classes from Basic One (BS 1) to Junior High School 2 (JHS 2) must write an entrance examination.

Step 3: Provide Previous School Enrollment Proof

Alongside the entrance examination, the parent/guardian needs to show proof of enrollment and class from the child's previous school.

Step 4: Initial Screening

After the entrance examination, if the child passes the initial screening process successfully, the parent/guardian proceeds to the next step.

Step 5: Payment of Fees

Upon successful screening, the parent/guardian is required to pay the full fees for the chosen class. Note that this payment does not cover school uniforms, books, and transport fare.

Note: Rare Admission into JHS 3

It's important to note that Agassi Memorial School rarely admits children into Junior High School 3.
