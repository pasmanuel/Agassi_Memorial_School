Welcome To Agassi Memorial School

Agassi Memorial School, a bastion of knowledge and a santuary for young minds, stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the heart of our community. Nestled amidst the lush greenery, this institution has been a nurturing ground for generations of students, each year adding to its legacy.

With its rich history dating back decades, Agassi Memorial School has consistently upheld its commitment to academic brilliance, character development, and the holistic growth of its students. The name "Agassi" has become synonymous with quality education, and it's a name that commands respect and admiration not only within our town but far beyond its borders.

Agassi
Preschool

PreSchool

In the Preschool, we recognize the profound impact early experiences have on a child's future. Our commitment extends beyond basic care to fostering a nurturing environment that positively influences a child's social, emotional, and cognitive development. By promoting a sense of security, trust, and joy, we aim to lay the groundwork for future academic success and lifelong learning.

Agassi
Primary School

Primary School

In the Primary, our goal is to make a lasting impact by nurturing well-rounded individuals. Beyond academic readiness, we focus on character development, emphasizing values such as empathy, kindness, and respect. By creating a supportive and inclusive environment, we empower our primary kids to become confident learners who positively impact their peers and community.

Junior High School

Junior High School

In the High School stream, we aim to foster a sense of responsibility and social consciousness. Our students are encouraged to apply their knowledge and skills to make a positive impact on their school community and beyond. Through service-learning projects, leadership opportunities, and community outreach, we empower our High school students to become compassionate, globally aware citizens who contribute meaningfully to the world around them.
