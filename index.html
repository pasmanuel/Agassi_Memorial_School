WELCOME, AKWAABA, BIENVENUE !!!

Welcome to Agassi Memorial School, your child’s hub of knowledge and a place for harnessing talents. Agassi Memorial School is a sanctuary for young minds and a beacon of hope to children who are often condemned as weak or useless in academics. It is a place where the child is the centre of learning in a serene and peaceful atmosphere.

Quality is our hallmark. We are a symbol of educational excellence in the heart of our community. Amidst the lush greenery of our environment, this institution has been a nurturing ground for great minds and adding on to its legacy each year. With its rich history, Agassi Memorial School has consistently upheld its commitment to academic brilliance, character development, and the total development of its students. The name "Agassi" has become synonymous with quality education, and it's a name that commands respect and admiration not only within Ghana but far beyond its borders. We welcome you and your ward on board this academic flight.

Field Trip 2024

The Agassi Day Out Field Trip 2024 was a remarkable event filled with educational experiences and delightful activities. This year’s field trip was designed to provide participants with an enriching day outside the classroom, fostering both learning and fun.
