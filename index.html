Welcome To Agassi Memorial School

Agassi Memorial School, a bastion of knowledge and a santuary for young minds, stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the heart of our community. Nestled amidst the lush greenery, this institution has been a nurturing ground for generations of students, each year adding to its legacy.

With its rich history dating back decades, Agassi Memorial School has consistently upheld its commitment to academic brilliance, character development, and the holistic growth of its students. The name "Agassi" has become synonymous with quality education, and it's a name that commands respect and admiration not only within our town but far beyond its borders.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMMES

PRE-SCHOOL

Agassi Memorial School Montessori program focuses on leveraging innate childhood traits to develop a child's full potential.

PRIMARY

Our Primary level of education consists of classes from Basic Stage (B.S.) 1 to 6 and a class size of thirty (30).

JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL

The Junior High School is a three-year program that prepares students for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) leading to the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE).

SMARTRIBE

The Smartribe program features Computer programming, Robotics, Abacus, and Verdic Maths.
